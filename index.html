Country_GDP Visualisations

This dataset has all the countries listed with their continents, GDP, population and GDP_per_capita.

This Vega-Lite specification creates an interactive geographic visualization that combines geographical shapes of countries with data on GDP per capita. It allows users to explore GDP per capita values for different countries by interacting with tooltips and the choropleth map. The gray fill represents countries with no available data.

This visualization provides valuable insights into the relationship between GDP per capita and population while also highlighting specific countries of interest.
